HTTP client 401 redirect flow

Unauthorized responses are handled centrally by the Axios response interceptor in src/api/httpClient.ts.

flowchart TD A[Axios response error] --> B{status == 401?} B -->|no| Z[Reject error] B -->|yes| C{Demo session?} C -->|yes| Z C -->|no| D{On public page?\n/ /login* /auth* /logout*} D -->|yes| Z D -->|no| E{Request is /api/me probe?} E -->|yes| Z E -->|no| F[Redirect to /login] F --> G[Stop handling for this request]

Notes: - /api/me is treated as a probe endpoint and should not force navigation. - Public pages avoid redirect loops and flicker.
